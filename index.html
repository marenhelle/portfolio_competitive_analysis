Portfolio competitive analysis

La moulade

Site URL http://lamoulade.com/
Date Looked At 01.19
Brand

Clean, colorful and fresh. I believe that the site in a successfull manner manages to stand out from most portfolios. It is consistent in its design and easy to understand.

Functionality

La Moulade shows off its content in a clear manner. Buttons could have been bigger to ease the navigation. It takes time to understand and get to the different pages, although there is a meny top right.

Interactivity

There is a wide use of transitions on the page. As you scroll down, different things are presented, as the company's services and portfolio. The navigation of the page might appear confusing, but this is balanced bay a simple graphical profile. The user is only in control of the scrolling, and is not able to choose between different sorting.

Navigation

At first it was difficult to understand the navigation concept of the page. It took some time before I figured that I was to scroll down to see the whole page. After I had used the page for some time, I felt like I got an overview of the system-architecture. At the first glance the layout may look like the 'Advanced Grid', but as one starts to scroll down, the navigation is quite unique.
